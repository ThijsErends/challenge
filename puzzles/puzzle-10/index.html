The Labyrinth of the 4-Digit Goat

Puzzle 10

I have a neck, but no head. I have a body, but no legs. I have a label, but no name. I am filled with a spirit, but I am not alive. What am I?

The 4-digit code is constructed as follows:
- The first digit is the number of the first puzzle.
- The second digit is the last digit of the number of the last puzzle.
- The third digit is the total number of puzzles, but take the last digit.
- The fourth digit is the number of letters in the answer to this riddle.
